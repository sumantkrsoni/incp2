About the Workshop

Finite Element Method (FEM) is the most powerful method for the analysis of engineering problems like Multiphysics problems. It is capable of handling geometrically complicated domains, a variety of boundary conditions, non-linearity, and coupled phenomena common in real-life problems.

This workshop is part of the Indo-Nor-Energy project, fostering collaboration between Indian institutions (IIT Jammu, IIT Delhi, CUSB Gaya) and the University of Bergen, Norway.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understanding of FEM mathematical foundations & solvers
  • Knowledge of error analysis techniques
  • Introduction to Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)
  • Practical experience with MATLAB/Python implementation

Topics to be Covered

  • Basics of functional analysis for FEM
  • Sobolev spaces and variational formulation
  • Finite element method for elliptic problems
  • Error analysis and adaptive methods
  • Implementation of FEM in MATLAB
  • Introduction to Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)
